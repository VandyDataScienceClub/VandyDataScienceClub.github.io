Engineering Managers

    Engineering Managers lead their teams of project developers to deliver data science and machine learning solutions for non-profit partners. They oversee project planning, ensure deadlines are met, and guide the technical direction of the project. This role is perfect for students with strong leadership skills and a passion for managing impactful, community-driven projects.

Project Developers

    Project Developers are the heart of the VDS teams, responsible for analyzing data, developing machine learning models, and building technical solutions. They work closely with the Engineering Manager and non-profit organizations to deliver data-driven insights that make a real-world impact. This role is ideal for students who want to apply and expand their technical skills in a collaborative environment.

Workshop Creators

    Workshop Creators dive deep into data science and machine learning topics, conducting research on a topic of interest to develop workshops for students in the Vanderbilt community. They design and lead sessions that teach students new skills and concepts in DS, fostering a learning environment for all experience levels. This role is suited for students who enjoy teaching and sharing knowledge in an interactive setting while learning new data science skills and concepts in the process.

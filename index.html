Welcome to Vanderbilt Data Science!

Vanderbilt Data Science (VDS) is a student-run organization for students who are passionate about leveraging data-driven solutions real-world problems impacting the community. We offer hands-on projects, workshops, and opportunities for students to collaborate and grow in the field of data science while learning essential skills for a successful career. Whether you're experienced in data science or just starting out, VDS welcomes you!

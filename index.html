Welcome to Vanderbilt Data Science!

Vanderbilt Data Science (VDS) is an organization for students who are passionate about leveraging data-driven solutions to solve real-world problems impacting the community. We offer hands-on projects, workshops, and opportunities for students to collaborate and grow while learning essential skills for a successful career in the data science field. Whether you're experienced in data science or just starting out, VDS welcomes you!
